Cardiology department


Cardiology is the branch of medicine that deals with the heart and blood vessels. It focuses on diagnosing and treating conditions such as heart disease, arrhythmias, heart failure, and hypertension. Cardiologists perform procedures like angioplasty, echocardiography, and cardiac surgery to restore heart health. Preventive care, including lifestyle management and early detection, is also a key part of cardiology. This department plays a vital role in saving lives by managing emergencies like heart attacks and providing long-term cardiac care.
