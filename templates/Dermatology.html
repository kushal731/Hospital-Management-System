Dermatology department


Dermatology is a specialized branch of medicine that focuses on diagnosing and treating conditions related to the skin, hair, and nails. It covers a wide range of issues, from common problems like acne, eczema, and fungal infections to more complex diseases such as psoriasis, vitiligo, and skin cancers. Dermatologists not only manage medical concerns but also provide cosmetic treatments, including chemical peels, laser therapy, and procedures to reduce scars or signs of aging. This field plays a crucial role in early detection of serious conditions like melanoma, ensuring timely intervention. By combining medical expertise with aesthetic care, dermatology helps patients maintain healthy skin while also enhancing confidence and overall well-being.
