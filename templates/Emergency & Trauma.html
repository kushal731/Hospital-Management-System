Emergency & Trauma department


Emergency & Trauma is a critical medical specialty that provides immediate care for patients suffering from accidents, injuries, and life-threatening conditions. It operates 24/7 to handle emergencies such as road traffic accidents, fractures, burns, cardiac arrests, and severe bleeding. Specialists in this field are trained to stabilize patients quickly, perform urgent interventions, and coordinate with other departments for advanced treatment. Trauma care often involves surgical procedures, intensive monitoring, and rapid decision-making to save lives. By delivering fast, efficient, and compassionate care, Emergency & Trauma services play a vital role in reducing mortality and ensuring patient recovery during critical situations.
