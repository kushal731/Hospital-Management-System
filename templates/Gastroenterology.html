Gastroenterology department


Gastroenterology is the branch of medicine that focuses on the digestive system, including the stomach, liver, pancreas, intestines, and related organs. It deals with conditions such as ulcers, gastritis, hepatitis, irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), and liver diseases. Gastroenterologists perform diagnostic procedures like endoscopy and colonoscopy to detect and treat disorders effectively. This specialty also emphasizes preventive care, dietary guidance, and long-term management of chronic digestive issues. By combining advanced technology with medical expertise, gastroenterology ensures better digestive health and overall well-being for patients.
